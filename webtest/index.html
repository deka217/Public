CHARACTER PROFILE

VIVI CHRONIA

People usually called her Vi, and she is very curious with AI. The Chronia family have a great reputation with so many AI inventions. However, her parents passed away in 2046 and she was left all alone. She is really grateful that her late parents invented Vision Man, an AI robot who will always be by her side. One day, both of them accidentally discovered her late father’s time machine in his abandoned lab. The thought of traveling to the past intrigued her, as she missed her parents so much. As a strong willed teenager, Vivi ensured herself and Vision Man to finally start their journey to meet her parents in 2023.
